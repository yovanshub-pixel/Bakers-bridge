FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
How does Bakers Bridge verify bakers? +
We personally visit every baker, taste their products, check their kitchen hygiene, verify their ingredients quality, and review their customer feedback before listing them on our platform.
Is it free for customers to use? +
Yes, completely free! Customers can search, browse, and contact bakers without any charges. We never take a commission from orders either.
How do I join as a home baker? +
Click "Join as a Baker" and fill in your details. Our team will contact you within 48 hours to schedule a quality inspection visit. Once verified, your profile goes live!
Do you take commission on orders? +
No commission at all. Customers contact bakers directly and all payments are handled between them. We only charge bakers a small monthly listing fee to maintain the platform.
What cities do you cover? +
We currently cover all major areas in Chennai. We are expanding to other Tamil Nadu cities soon. Stay tuned!
What is included in the photography package? +
Our photography package includes a professional photoshoot of your baked goods, edited photos optimized for social media and your profile, and a digital delivery of all images within 5 working days.
