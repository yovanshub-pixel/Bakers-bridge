FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
How does Bakers Bridge verify bakers? +
We personally visit every baker, taste their products, check kitchen hygiene, verify ingredient quality, and review customer feedback before listing them.
Is it free for customers to use? +
Yes, completely free! Customers can search, browse, and contact bakers without any charges. We never take a commission from orders either.
How do I join as a home baker? +
Click "Join as a Baker" and fill in your details. Our team will contact you within 48 hours to schedule a quality inspection visit. Once verified, your profile goes live!
Do you take commission on orders? +
No commission at all. Customers contact bakers directly and all payments are handled between them. We only charge bakers a small monthly listing fee.
What cities do you cover? +
We currently cover all major areas in Chennai and are expanding to other Tamil Nadu cities soon!
